Introduction

Department of Physics from the School of Basic Science at Manipal University Jaipur, Rajasthan is going to organize a three day inter-national conference on multi-functional nano materials, from 28th - 30th December, 2020. Due to the current ongoing global COVID-19 pandemic and prioritizing the motto 'Health is weath and it's better to take precaution than cure.' The mode of conference is online via Microsoft Teams.

We intend to bring together leading academic scientists, researchers, and scholars to share their experience and research on all aspects and vibrant applications of multi-functional nano materials. It also provides a premier interdisciplinary platform for all to present and discuss the recent innovation, trend, and concerns as well as plactical challenges encountered and counter solution adopted in the field of material science. The online platform will be made available to all enrolled only. There is no registration fee for this conference and registration is done via dedicated online page only.

Multifunctional nanomaterials exhibit greater properties over conventional materials, serving several goals synergistically. They are gradually attracting more and more attention because of their ability to accomodate numerous properties, as electronic, magnetic, optical, and catalytic. Their highly functional behaviour with modified properties is often observed to be quite vibrant from to those of the individual materials. The distinctive properties of these materials employed together with other materials or through functionalization, meet the diverse application requirements. The purpose of the functionalization is manyfold, such as surface modification, ability to increase the functionality, dispersibility, and stability, controlled release of single nanoparticle, reduction in consumption of materials, etc. Therefore, it is an appropriate time to open a forum where a high quality and an exceptional knowledge exchange can take place among students, academics and industry researchers.
